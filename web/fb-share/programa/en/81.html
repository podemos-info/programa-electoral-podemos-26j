Abandonment of the policy of wage devaluation as a way of promoting an improvement in competitiveness

We will abandon the policy of wage devaluation as a way of promoting competitiveness because of its negative social and economic effects. To do this, we will repeal the last two labour reform laws and will adopt measures to promote sufficient growth of wages and improve purchasing power. Also we will strengthen the role of collective bargaining by repealing the measures included in the most recent labour reform. We will increase the minimum wage and end wage cuts in the public sector.
