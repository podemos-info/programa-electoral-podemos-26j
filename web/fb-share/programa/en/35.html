Guaranteeing access to science culture

We will guarantee access to scientific culture regardless of personal, economic or functional autonomy situations:

  • Science museums will be free one day a month, to ensure socially-inclusive access.
  • We will ensure that all research centres, museums, fairs and other scientific events are accessible to persons with a range of mobility difficulties.
  • We will promote open door events in all research centres and universities, aimed at citizens of all ages.
  • Public media will offer rigorous and appropriate content; this will help establish strong links between society and the scientific community. Furthermore, we will provide spaces for dissemination and scientific debate, ensuring that these are appropriate to the age range of the target audience.
