Support of clusters to reverse the process of deindustrialization

We will reverse the process of deindustrialization, promote a new productive diversification and strengthen an innovative production network focused on the development of SMEs, identifying those emerging sectors connected to network industries.

Clusters, defined as basic instruments of cooperation among the most dynamic companies in a sector and public administrations, research institutions and universities should become useful tools to extend good practices, encourage innovation and improve overall productivity of companies in the sector. For this reason, we will promote a new connection between global and local spheres by promoting existing industrial clusters, their relationship with regional development platforms and the extension of good practices that already exist in some of them.
