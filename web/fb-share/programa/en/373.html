Removal, burial or transfer of the two power lines which currently cross the town of Arroyomolinos from north to south, above housing, parks, playgrounds and sports areas

The steps we will take to achieve it are as follows:

  • The development and diffusion of a study of the whole process (project definition, installation and environmental impact results).
  • Coordination with neighborhood platforms and civil society, as well as experts and authorities involved, to determine the best alternative (removal, burial or transfer).
  • Authorization to carry out the agreed solution.
