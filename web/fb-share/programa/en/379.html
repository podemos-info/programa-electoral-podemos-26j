Dismantling the shooting range in Bardenas Reales

We will convert the –Bardenas shooting range into a natural environment for the enjoyment of the people of Navarra. To do this, it will be necessary:

  • To call a referendum to decide on the renewal of the lease contract.
  • Remove the denomination of "area of interest for defence"
  • Proceed with the dismantling of the range.
  • Perform an environmental and sanitary analysis of the area.
  • Ensure the reuse of natural resources for social, cultural and ecological purposes.
