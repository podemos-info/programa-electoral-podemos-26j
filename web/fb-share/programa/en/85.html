We will promote the reform of European institutions to democratize political and economic decisions in the Eurozone

We will promote reform of the European institutions to democratise political and economic decisions in the Eurozone. The economic government of the Eurozone -the Eurogroup- is an entity that is not required to be accountable to any institution directly elected by citizens, not even the European Parliament. Therefore we will support the creation of a parliamentary chamber in the Eurozone, made up of representatives from the different national parliaments according to demographic and territorial criteria, which will have, unlike the European Parliament, real legislative power and political control.
