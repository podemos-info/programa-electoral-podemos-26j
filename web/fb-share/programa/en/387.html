Support agriculture, livestock farming and fishing

We will apply a 20% complement, charged against the National Budget funds allocated to the Valencia Region, to the following areas:

  • Agricultural research and technology
  • Improvement and regulation of the fishing industry
  • Agricultural guarantee and support
  • Concentration of supply and co-operativism
  • Agrifood quality
  • Rural development
  • Livestock development and improvements
  • Water resources administration and infrastructures
  • Treatment and sanitation of water systems and farming infrastructures
