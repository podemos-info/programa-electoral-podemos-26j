Army members: citizens and workers

We will reform the 9/ 2011 27th July Army Members’ Rights and Duties Law, in order to promote a new regulation of fundamental rights such as freedom of assembly, right of gathering, right to protest, right of association, freedom of speech, right to unionize, and right to affiliate to political parties.

We will also eliminate deprivation of freedom without effective judicial protection, and will renew the bodies for participation according to democratic criteria, that will also be binding when it comes to law-making processes in the professional, social, and economic spheres.
