Independent Justice for the Military

We will reform the Statutory Law of Judicial Power in order to create bodies which are specialized in matters of military discipline and punishment, carried out by judges and magistrates with appropriate training and integrated into the jurisdiction orders of administrative-contention and penal law. We will also suppress Room V of the Military Supreme Court for reasons of economy, independence and transparency. The present make-up of the military justice system does not guarantee its full independence from military command.
