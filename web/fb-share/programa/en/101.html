Guaranteed Income

We will create a programme of differential benefits (supplementary to existing income) for all households with an income level below the poverty line. To do this, we will increase the amount of basic benefit per person in the family unit. Initially, this amount will be 600 Euros per month per family unit with one member and it will then be increased according to the number of family members (an additional 35% of guaranteed income for the second member, and 20% for each subsequent family member) up to a maximum of 1290 Euros. This plan includes all social benefits which are below this threshold.
