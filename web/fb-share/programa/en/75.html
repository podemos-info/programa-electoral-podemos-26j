Promotion of entrepreneurship in innovative sectors

We will support and promote entrepreneurship in innovative sectors, particularly in the digital economy, by promoting hubs (small business centres) and innovation clusters. We will encourage access to workspaces and co-working (collaborative work) the incubators of entrepreneurs and business start-ups, to be able to create a network of collaboration spaces that enable small entrepreneurs to pool resources, provide shared services and facilitate innovation in a decentralized way, so that the general public and the productive sectors may benefit. We will promote public funding in these sectors and public-private partnership to produce digital applications of general interest. On the other hand, we will regulate the collaborative economy to provide a clear and also legal framework; we will encourage the incorporation of services and collaborative digital economy by the government.
