Against tax evasion, "Tobin tax" and global tax on wealth

We will urge the creation of an international tax agency under the auspices of the UN, to combat tax evasion or tax competition between countries that have sanctioning capacity and we will require multinational corporations to pay their taxes where they generate profits. The agency will aim:

  • To study the creation of a global wealth tax to gradually tax large blocks of wealth as a guarantee of social rights.
  • The adoption of a tax on financial transactions.
  • Fixing a universal definition and binding rules for tax havens.
