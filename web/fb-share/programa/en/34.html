Supporting senior researchers

  • We will increase by 100% the number of central Government postdoctoral contracts.
  • We will promote, for top researchers at national level, indefinite contracts subject to evaluation.
  • We will widen the list of receiving centres, to include, besides the PRIs, universities and other national centres of excellence.
  • We will define and implement an evaluation model for such institutions.
  • We will widen the dissemination and impact of their investigations.
